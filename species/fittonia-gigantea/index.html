Watering

You should water your Fittonia gigantea when the top layer of the soil is dry. Feel the soil by pushing a finger an inch or so below the surface. If the soil is still moist, no further water is needed.

Use a well-draining pot and make sure the roots of the plant do not stand in water in order to avoid root rot.
